The Problem with Shift Scheduling

Shift managers in hospitals, factories, warehouses, and security teams routinely assign shifts without an easy way to see whether a schedule is pushing an employee into fatigue territory.

Too little rest, too many consecutive days, too many night shifts, or accidental double-booking can lead to critical safety risks. This system automatically flags these risks.

Our Solution

Our API gives managers visibility automatically, every time a shift is created or reviewed. We combine a deterministic rule engine with an Anthropic AI layer that explains why a schedule is risky and suggests safer alternatives.
